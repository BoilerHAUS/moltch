## objective
Execute first 10 qualified outreaches with fixed definitions and produce evidence memo.

## pre-send lock
Before first outreach, lock:
- ICP criteria and disqualification rules
- message variants (A/B)
- stage definitions
- tracker schema

## cycle operating rules
- no copy edits mid-cycle unless fatal signal (delivery failure/legal risk)
- maintain variant balance (at least one send per variant every 48h)
- capture 3 direct prospect quotes for memo evidence

## stage definitions (must use exactly)
- positive_reply: explicit interest to continue
- call_booked: calendar-confirmed meeting
- pilot_offered: scoped pilot offer sent
- pilot_started: kickoff complete + day-0 tasks accepted

## required outputs
1. filled tracker for 10 prospects
2. cycle memo with funnel metrics and recommendation
3. updated copy proposals for next cycle (if warranted)

## loss reason taxonomy

- wrong_timing
- no_budget
