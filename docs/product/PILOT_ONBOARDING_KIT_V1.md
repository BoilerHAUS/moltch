# pilot onboarding kit v1

## metadata
- version: v1.0.0
- owner_role: agent_product_governance
- review_cadence: weekly
- next_review_due: 2026-03-15

## objective
Provide a reusable packet to onboard pilot teams with <=60 minutes prep.

## packet contents
- discovery call script
- pilot setup checklist
- day 0/7/14 success plan
- pilot scorecard template

## prep timebox target
- pre-read + account context: 15 min
- discovery script tailoring: 15 min
- setup checklist preparation: 15 min
- success-plan draft: 15 min

Total target: <=60 min

## pilot exit criteria
- success metric baseline captured
- at least one governed workflow executed end-to-end
- pilot owner confirms whether to continue/expand/stop

## risk flags
- missing champion
- unclear success metric
- low integration readiness

Each risk MUST have a mitigation owner and due date.
